Overview

Art is a crucial aspect of human history that has lasted from early civilizations
to the modern day. It helps the Louvre attract around 10 million visitors per
year, and has resulted in world-famous works such as Mona Lisa and the Statue of Liberty.

Art is so influential that it was even considered an event during the 1912-1948 Olympic Games.
It's produced countless famous and unknown artists, and is taught in schools around the
country, from the tiniest kindergarteners to collegiate students in graduate
school. This website aims to open the reader's eyes to the wonderful world of art.
