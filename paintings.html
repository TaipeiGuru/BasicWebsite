Many, many paintingss have been created throughout the course of history. However, not all artists are skilled enough to ensure that their work lasts for centuries. Here are two of the most popular and recognizable paintings worldwide.


Mona Lisa


Quite possibly the most famous painting in human history, the Mona Lisa was painted by Leonardo da Vinci around 1502. He modeled it after a woman named Lisa Gherardini, the wife of an Italian cloth merchant. The painting currently resides at The Louvre, in Paris, France. It has helped The Louvre become one of the most visited museums in recent years.



The Last Supper


Another world-famous classic, The Last Supper was painted by Leonardo da Vinci between 1495 and 1498. It features Jesus Christ and the 12 apostles, and can be found at the Santa Maria delle Grazie monastery in Milan, Italy. The painting (technically a fresco) has undergone numerous restoration projects over the centuries in order to preserve the masterpiece.
